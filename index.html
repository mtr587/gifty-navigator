🔍 The Great Gift Mismatch

Christmas preview: what people give vs. what people actually want

🎁 Most Given (Giver's Picks)

  1. Clothing & Accessories — 52% of givers
  2. Gift Cards — 46%
  3. Toys — 36%
  4. Books & Media — 33%
  5. Personal Care / Beauty — 28%
VS

🎯 Most Wanted (Recipient's Wishlist)

  1. Cash — 65-70% want it
  2. Gift Cards — 55-60%
  3. Clothing (if it fits my style) — 40-45%
  4. Electronics — 35-40%
  5. Experiences (concerts, travel) — 30-35%
💡

The Insight: Clothing is the #1 gift given but only works when it perfectly matches personal style — and it often doesn't. Gift cards are the rare overlap where both sides agree. The biggest gap? Experiences — givers rarely give them, but recipients consistently rank them in their top 5. See this pattern for every occasion in our full guides below.
