💡 Baby Shower Gift Tips

1. Check the registry first. Parents spend hours picking items. If there's a registry, buy from it. It's the safest bet.
2. Think beyond newborn. Buy diapers in sizes 2-4, clothes in 6-12 months. Parents will remember your foresight.
3. Meal delivery is gold. A $50 DoorDash gift card beats a $50 plush toy every single time. New parents don't cook.
4. Limit stuffed animals to ONE. If you must gift a plushie, make it the only one they get. Better yet, pair it with a practical item.
5. Go in on a group gift. For close friends, organize a group contribution toward the stroller or car seat — high-impact, appreciated.
