💡 Easter Gift Tips

1. Spring is the real theme. Beyond candy, Easter is about the arrival of spring. Outdoor toys, gardening kits, and spring clothing all fit perfectly.
2. Quality over sugar overload. One artisan chocolate bunny beats a pound of cheap candy. Upgrade the basket rather than overfilling it.
3. For older kids: digital eggs. Cash, gift cards, or download codes in plastic eggs make the egg hunt genuinely exciting for teens.
4. Craft kits > plush toys. An activity they do (egg decorating, spring crafts) creates more lasting joy than a stuffed animal.
