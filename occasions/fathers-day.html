💡 Father's Day Gift Tips (Based on Data)

1.

Stop buying ties. Clothing is the #3 most-given Father's Day category at 43%, but ties are the most-regifted item in history. Dads rank hobby gear 5x higher than neckties on their wish list.

2.

Hobby gear is the sweet spot. If he grills, golfs, fishes, or woodworks — upgrade his kit. The dad who 'has everything' doesn't have the premium version of his favorite tool yet.

3.

Electronics are under-given. Only 24% of givers buy electronics, but it's a top-2 most-wanted category. A wireless thermometer, smart home gadget, or new tech accessory bridges the gap.

4.

An outing with him beats an item for him. 49% of givers plan a special outing — and dads love it. Make it a shared experience: ballgame, fishing trip, or a round of golf together.
