💡 Housewarming Gift Tips

1. Smart home upgrades > decor. A video doorbell, smart plug kit, or smart lights work in any home regardless of style.
2. Consumables are always safe. Wine, olive oil, coffee, chocolate — no style risk, no dust collection.
3. Ask about the neighborhood. A gift card to a great local restaurant helps them explore their new community.
4. Skip the plant if they're not plant people. A dead plant two weeks later is worse than no plant at all.
