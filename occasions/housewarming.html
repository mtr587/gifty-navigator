The Insight: Never buy decorative items before homeowners establish their style — it probably won't match. Plants die with neglectful owners. Gift cards to home improvement stores are the safest, most appreciated housewarming gift. If you must bring a physical item, make it a consumable (wine, olive oil, coffee).

💡 Housewarming Gift Tips

1. Gift cards > decor. Home Depot, Target, or Wayfair gift cards let homeowners choose what fits their space.
2. Consumables are always safe. Wine, olive oil, coffee, chocolate — no style risk, no dust collection.
3. Ask about the neighborhood. A gift card to a great local restaurant helps them explore their new community.
4. Skip the plant if they're not plant people. A dead plant two weeks later is worse than no plant at all.
