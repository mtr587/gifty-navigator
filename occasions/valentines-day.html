💡 Valentine's Day Gift Tips (Based on Data)

1.

Skip the giant teddy bear. 57% of people give candy and stuffed animals, but adults overwhelmingly prefer experiences and meaningful gifts. A teddy bear collects dust; a spa day creates memories.

2.

Dinner out is the sweet spot. Both givers (34%) and receivers love the evening-out category. Make it special with a reservation at a place they've been wanting to try — bonus points for booking it yourself.

3.

Jewelry turns heads (literally). Only 21% of givers buy jewelry, but it's one of the most-wanted categories. You don't need to spend $500 — a thoughtful birthstone piece at $50–100 carries the same emotional weight.

4.

Personalization is the cheat code. A $30 personalized star map outperforms a $50 generic gift basket every time. The effort you put into customization signals thoughtfulness.
